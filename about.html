Coffee is one of the three most popular beverages in the world (alongside water and tea), and it is one of the most profitable international commodities.

Coffee is much more than a morning ritual – it's a passion, a global culture, and a multi-billion dollar. From its rich history to the perfect brewing techniques, here’s everything you need to know about coffee.Coffee’s journey began in the highlands of Ethiopia, where, according to legend, a goat herder named Kaldi discovered the energizing effects of coffee beans. He noticed his goats became unusually lively after eating the red berries of a particular tree. Fast forward centuries, and coffee spread to the Arabian Peninsula, where it became an integral part of Middle Eastern culture. By the 17th century, coffee had reached Europe, eventually becoming a global drink cherished by millions. Coffee is a versatile beverage that pairs well with a variety of foods. If you’re a fan of breakfast or dessert pairings, try these suggestions: Cappuccino: Pairs beautifully with pastries,such as croissants, scones, or biscotti. The frothy milk complements the richness of buttery treats. Espresso: Pairs well with dark chocolate, nuts, or a simple biscotti for a more intense flavor experience. Iced Coffee: Try with fruit-based pastries like lemon scones or berry muffins for a refreshing contrast.
